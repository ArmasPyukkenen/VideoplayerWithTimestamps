Custom Videoplayer with possibility to make notes on certain timestamps

Here is what you can do with it:

  • You can watch a file from your local computer with this player. For that drag and drop a file into the specified zone below.
  • If you just want to evaluate the player, an example video is preloaded.
  • You can mark the current moment in the video by clicking the clock icon on the control panel. The yellow marker will appear on the progress bar.
  • Click on the yellow circle to open the timestamp editor window. There you can edit your note, rewind video to the respective moment or delete the timestamp.
